Design Explanation

1
Purple primary palette (#6C5CE7) chosen for its association with knowledge, creativity, and learning environments — distinct from typical red/blue edu-apps
2
Gamification layer (XP, streaks, badges) drives daily retention by making learning progress visually rewarding and habit-forming
3
Video player with inline notes and quick-check quiz keeps learning active, not passive — increasing knowledge retention
4
Progress bars on every course card create Zeigarnik effect — users psychologically want to complete started tasks
5
All screens navigable via bottom nav AND inline taps — two navigation patterns reduce getting "stuck"
